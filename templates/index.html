About This Application

This is detection of anomalies in Electrocardiogram (ECG) signals using deep learning techniques, specifically Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) autoencoders. ECG signals are vital indicators of heart health, and the detection of irregularities such as arrhythmias is critical for early diagnosis and treatment of cardiac conditions.

We leverage LSTM autoencoders to learn the normal patterns of ECG signals, and when an input deviates significantly from these learned patterns, it is flagged as an anomaly. LSTMs are particularly suited for this task due to their ability to capture long-term dependencies in sequential data, making them ideal for modeling the temporal dynamics of ECG signals.

Our solution is designed to assist healthcare professionals by providing an automated system that can quickly and accurately identify abnormal heart rhythms, enabling timely intervention. Through this project, we aim to enhance cardiac health monitoring and contribute to the advancement of AI-driven medical diagnostics.
